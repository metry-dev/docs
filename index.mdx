Metry gives you a simple, reliable way to track what your users do — from their first page view to conversion and beyond. With a handful of method calls, you can identify users, record named events, and stream batched data to your analytics ingest endpoint without blocking the browser.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install the SDK">
    Add `@metry-dev/sdk` to your project with npm, yarn, or pnpm.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Initialize with your API key">
    Call `Metry.init()` once with your publishable API key and ingest endpoint URL.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Identify your users">
    Call `Metry.identify()` after login to attach a user ID and traits to all subsequent events.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Track events">
    Call `Metry.step()` anywhere in your app to record named events. Metry batches and flushes them automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Metry is browser-only. The package has no import-time side effects, but `init()` and all tracking methods should only be called from client-side code.
</Note>
